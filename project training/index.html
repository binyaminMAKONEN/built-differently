9 Easy Healthy Dinner Recipes

Air-Fryer Fish

We love the classic fish and chip combo and normally turn to our favorite beer battered fish when the craving hits. But frying can be messy and sometimes we'd like to skip that part—this air-fryer fish is the perfect substitute. It is every bit as crunchy and the fish stays perfectly flaky too.

Get the Air-Fryer Fish recipe.

Salmon Tacos

The first thing you might think of when you hear the words "fish tacos" are probably Baja-style tacos filled with crispy fried fish. But tacos can be made with all manner of marine life, from shrimp to salmon! These tacos are cinch to make and are fresh, zesty, healthy, and absolutely delicious.

Get the Salmon Tacos recipe.

Stuffed Acorn Squash

All our favorite fall flavors come together in this surprisingly easy and super flavorful stuffed acorn squash. The kale adds a heartiness that only a good cruciferous green can provide, and it pairs perfectly with the tender, sweet flesh of the squash. Yum!

Get the Stuffed Acorn Squash recipe.

Garlicky Lemon Mahi Mahi

If you haven’t tried mahi mahi, you really need to get on that. It’s a firm, mild fish that when cooked is beautifully flaky and tender. Our favorite way to prep it? Cook it on the stove with some fresh spring asparagus, and then smother it in a lemony butter sauce.

Get the Garlicky Lemon Mahi Mahi recipe.

Healthy Chicken Casserole

This satisfying casserole has ALL of your fall favorites—Brussels sprouts, butternut squash, cranberries... Make it for a big crowd, or pack it up and eat the leftovers for lunch. It's a meal prep DREAM.

Get the Harvest Chicken Casserole recipe.

Honey Garlic Glazed Salmon

A longtime favorite of Delish readers, this savory-sweet salmon never disappoints. Hot tip: Let the fillets cook undisturbed for a few minutes. That, and super high heat is how you get that delicious crust here.

Get the Honey Garlic Glazed Salmon recipe.

Cauliflower Crust Pizza

Possibly our favorite use of cauliflower as a culinary master of disguise is in pizza crust. It might sound crazy but it can be a delicious and healthy alternative to the classic.

Get the Cauliflower Crust Pizza recipe.

Seared Ahi Tuna & Arugula Pear Salad

We love using a slightly spicy green to counter the fatty tuna here, like arugula! It has a deliciously peppery flavor that pairs well with the quality sushi-grade ahi tuna in this salad.

Get the Seared Ahi Tuna & Arugula Pear Salad recipe.

Grilled Chicken Salad

Cucumbers, tomato, feta, olives, and avocado combine to create a salad that may just be your go to office lunch from here on out. Hot tip for taking this salad to go: Ditch the whisking and throw all the dressing ingredients into a jar or small tupperware. Right before you eat, give the jar a good shake and you will have a perfectly emulsified vinaigrette.

Get the Grilled Chicken Salad recipe.
